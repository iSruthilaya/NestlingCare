Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 bunches of spinach (palak), blanched and pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them crackle.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chili, and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and the oil separates.
  5. Stir in turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for a minute.
  6. Add the spinach puree and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Stir in the paneer cubes and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well. Add fresh cream for extra richness.
  9. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
