Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas (or canned, drained and rinsed)
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup diced tomato
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chickpeas, cucumber, tomato, onion, and cilantro.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Chill the salad for about 30 minutes before serving for best flavor.
  5. Serve as a light meal or a side dish with your favorite main course.
