Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or almond milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • 1/2 cup water or ice cubes

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine oats, banana, yogurt (or almond milk), and cinnamon powder.
  2. Add honey for sweetness (if desired) and water or ice cubes to adjust consistency.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy the smoothie fresh as a healthy breakfast or snack.
