Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup fresh fenugreek leaves (methi), finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon oil (for the dough)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine whole wheat flour, fenugreek leaves, yogurt, turmeric powder, cumin seeds, red chili powder, coriander powder, oil, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually and knead into a soft dough. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each one into a thin circle.
  4. Heat a tawa or skillet over medium heat and cook each thepla with a little oil on both sides until golden brown.
  5. Serve warm with yogurt, pickle, or chutney.
