Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup moong dal (split yellow lentils)
  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 small onion, chopped (optional)
  • 1 small tomato, chopped (optional)
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the moong dal and rice under running water until the water runs clear.
  2. Heat ghee in a pressure cooker or a deep pan over medium heat.
  3. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped onion, green chili, and tomato (if using), and saut√© until soft.
  5. Add turmeric powder and the rinsed moong dal and rice. Stir to combine.
  6. Pour in 3 cups of water, add salt, and mix well.
  7. Cover and cook for 3-4 whistles in a pressure cooker or until the dal and rice are soft if cooking in a pan.
  8. Once the pressure releases, stir the khichdi to mix everything well.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve warm with ghee or yogurt.
