Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup boiled peas
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • Water as needed
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix whole wheat flour with water to form a soft dough. Set aside for 15-20 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, mix grated carrots, mashed potatoes, chopped onions, boiled peas, garam masala, cumin powder, red chili powder, salt, and coriander leaves.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll out each ball into a small circle.
  4. Place a spoonful of the vegetable mixture in the center of the circle, then fold the edges over to seal.
  5. Gently roll the stuffed dough ball into a flat paratha.
  6. Heat a griddle or tawa over medium heat, and cook the paratha with oil or ghee until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Serve hot with yogurt, pickle, or chutney.
