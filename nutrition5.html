Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup grated beetroot
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, cashews)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan, add grated carrots and beetroot, and saut√© for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add milk and cook on low heat until the mixture thickens, stirring occasionally.
  3. Once the milk is absorbed, add sugar and stir until it dissolves completely.
  4. Add cardamom powder, saffron, and chopped nuts, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat when the halwa reaches the desired consistency.
  6. Garnish with extra nuts and serve warm.
