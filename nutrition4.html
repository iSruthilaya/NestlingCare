Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup diced potatoes
  • 1/2 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 cloves, 1 cinnamon stick, 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the basmati rice thoroughly and soak for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds, cloves, cinnamon, and cardamom. Sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add diced carrots, peas, potatoes, and green beans. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Stir in the drained rice and sauté for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add garam masala, salt, and water. Stir well.
  7. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is fully cooked and the water is absorbed.
  8. Fluff the pulao with a fork and serve hot.
