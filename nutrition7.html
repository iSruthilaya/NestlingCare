Ingredients

  • 1 cup masoor dal (red lentils)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the masoor dal thoroughly and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and add cumin seeds. Let them sizzle for a few seconds.
  3. Add chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until the onion is golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomato, turmeric, garam masala, coriander, and salt. Cook until the tomato softens.
  5. Add the rinsed masoor dal and water, and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the dal is soft and cooked through.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or chapati.
