Ingredients

  • 1 cup ragi flour
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix ragi flour, rice flour, curd, salt, and enough water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Add chopped onion, green chili, cumin seeds, and asafoetida to the batter. Mix well.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan or tawa and grease it lightly with oil.
  4. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  5. Drizzle a few drops of oil around the edges and cook on medium heat until the dosa turns crispy.
  6. Flip and cook the other side for a few seconds.
  7. Serve hot with chutney or sambar.
