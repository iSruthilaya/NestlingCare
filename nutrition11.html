Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves, chopped
  • 1 medium carrot, peeled and chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic and sauté until soft.
  2. Add chopped carrots and sauté for another 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add cumin powder, black pepper, and salt. Stir well.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat, cover the pan, and let it simmer for 10-12 minutes until the carrots are tender.
  6. Add the chopped spinach and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Turn off the heat and blend the soup using a hand blender until smooth.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cream if desired.
